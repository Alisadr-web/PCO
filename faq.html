Student FAQs

  • How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
    You need to log in to the HZ print system and send your file to the printer queue. Then use your student card to release it at any printer.
  • How can you scan a document at HZ?
    Use the multifunction printer, place your document, and select “Scan to email” option. It will send the file to your HZ email.
  • How can I buy something in the HZ web shop?
    Log in to HZ Webshop with your student account and choose your product.
  • How can you book a project space?
    Use the MyHZ portal to book a project room.
  • What are the parking instructions at HZ?
    Register your car license plate in the student portal and follow the signs to the HZ parking lot.
